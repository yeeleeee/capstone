Our Team How does it work? Why LOOP?

LOOP is a quiz publishing workflow that accommodates the needs of the scalable university model developed at Western Governors University. The application creates learning effectiveness with a focus on enabling cross-silo collaboration.

  • bring curricular elements into shared spaces to reduce redundancy and improve quality
  • improved information flow about effectiveness of learning content,
  • improved collaboration between Academic Programs and course instructors,
  • improved buy-in among course instructors for course materials
  • improved learning outcomes for our students
