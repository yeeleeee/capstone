Our Team How does it work? Why LOOP?
Clockwise from left: Ye Jin Lee (designer), Courtney Francis (coordinator), Mengxin Yu (content and creative lead), Pankaj Ajit (developer), Teja Talluri (researcher)

We are graduate students in the METALS program at Carnegie Mellon University. We apply learning science principles, evidence-based research, and data analysis to design effective educational technologies within formal and informal settings. The program culminates with a seven-month capstone project for an external client.

Our team has work experience in Education Technology Design, Marketing, Data Science, Machine Learning, Cognitive Psychology, Information Management, and Computer Science.
