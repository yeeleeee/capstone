This publishing workflow serves WGU instructors and academic programs departments in supporting student learning with data-driven quizzes that can be included in course study guides or delivered directly to students with specific needs.

LOOP includes options for quiz and question creation to satisfy different working relationships within WGU.

Optional feedback options include asynchronous comments on questions and quizzes and visibility and insight from other areas within WGU.

  • Provides students with test-enhanced learning opportunities
